Meet Yashvi

Yashvi is a dedicated and empathetic psychologist with a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology. She believes in creating a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings freely.

Her approach is client-centered, integrating evidence-based therapies like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Mindfulness to create a personalized treatment plan. She is committed to helping you develop coping strategies, build resilience, and achieve a more balanced and fulfilling life.
